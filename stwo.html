Choose Dog Dicord Bot_1.0:

After the learning more about discord bot through the building of the two discord bots above, I decided to create a discord bot of my preference. I want to create a discord bot that can give user suggestions on what breeds of dog the user might like to get. The user can awake the bot by typing '!choose_dog', then the bot will ask user three questions. The first question is the color of the dog user may like, giving with the choices of Black, White or Brown. The second questioin is the size of the dog, giving with the choices of Small, Medium and Large. Lastly, the third question is the activity level of the dog, giving with the choices of Low, Medium, and High. The user will type in the answer for each question. After all three questions have been answered, the bot will give out three dog breeds suggestion. However, some combination of color, size and activity level might not have suggestion.
